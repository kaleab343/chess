## 🎮 How to Use Interactive Lessons

### Step 1: Access the Tutorial
1. Open your browser to `http://localhost:5174/`
2. Click the big purple button: **"📚 Open Chess Pattern Bible"**

### Step 2: Choose a Lesson
- Browse by category (Opening, Tactics, Middlegame, Endgame)
- Each card shows:
  - 📝 Number of interactive positions
  - ❓ Number of quiz questions
- Click any lesson to start

### Step 3: Play Through the Lesson
1. **Read the instruction** for the current position
2. **Drag and drop** a chess piece to make your move
3. **Get instant feedback**:
   - ✅ **Green** = Correct! See explanation why it works
   - ❌ **Red** = Try again! Hints what to look for
4. **Click "Next Step"** to continue
5. **Complete all positions** in the sequence

### Step 4: Take the Quiz
- After finishing the lesson, test yourself
- No hints this time!
- Track your accuracy score
- Aim for 80%+ to show mastery
